## ❓ 常見問題 FAQ

### Q: 我是社工/心理師個人使用,要付錢嗎?
### Q: I'm a social worker / therapist using this personally — do I need to pay?

**A:** 不用。個人實務使用永遠免費,您只需要遵守 AGPL-3.0(基本上「不要把工具改成閉源賣錢」)。
**A:** No. Personal practice use is always free under AGPL-3.0 (you basically just can't fork it into a closed-source paid product).

---

### Q: 我們是非營利組織(教會 / NGO / 學校),要付錢嗎?
### Q: We're a non-profit organization (church / NGO / school) — do we need to pay?

**A:** 非營利使用免費(AGPL-3.0)。若您需要客製修改且不希望開源您的修改,則需商業授權。
**A:** Non-profit use is free under AGPL-3.0. If you need custom modifications and don't want to open-source those changes, then commercial license applies.

---

### Q: 我們公司想把這工具嵌入 EHR 系統賣給醫院,可以嗎?
### Q: Can our company embed this tool in an EHR system sold to hospitals?

**A:** 可以,但需要商業授權(因為 EHR 是商業閉源產品)。歡迎來信洽談。
**A:** Yes, but you'll need a commercial license (since EHR is a commercial closed-source product). Contact us to discuss.

---

### Q: 我貢獻 Pull Request,會被收錢嗎?
### Q: If I contribute a Pull Request, will I be charged?

**A:** 不會。Pull Request 採 inbound = outbound 原則(您的貢獻以 AGPL-3.0 授權給專案)。
PR 被合併後,該段程式碼也納入未來商業授權交付範圍(視同您將著作權貢獻給專案)。
**A:** No. PRs follow the inbound = outbound rule (your contribution is licensed under AGPL-3.0 to the project). Once merged, the contributed code is also part of future commercial license deliveries (treated as a copyright contribution to the project).

---

### Q: 商業授權費用多少?
### Q: How much does the commercial license cost?

**A:** 視貴單位規模、使用範圍、客製需求而定,沒有固定價目表。
小型營利機構 vs 跨國企業 vs 政府客製案,差距很大。請來信討論。
**A:** Depends on organization size, scope of use, and customization needs — no fixed price list.
Small for-profit org vs multinational enterprise vs government custom project vary significantly. Please contact us to discuss.
